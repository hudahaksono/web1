White Opium Bird

Yes, that’s right! The opium bird is a hoax. In 2023, a video of a bird that looked like it was smoking a pipe went viral on social media. The bird was dubbed the "Opium Bird" and many people believed that it was a real animal. However, it was later revealed that the video was actually a hoax. The bird in the video was a CGI creation and the pipe was added in post-production
